About Höppner

Höppner understands that not every web developer is an expert photographer. Finding good quality stock photos for your projects can often be impossible unless you want to break the bank. That's why we decided to start Höppner!

At Höppner, we understand that not every developer wants to spend huge sums of money on photos for their projects. We also found that there aren't many free options that provide a wide variety of good quality photos.

We are here to fill that gap in the market by providing a large amount of high quality photos at an affordable subscription fee. If subscriptions aren't your thing, we also provide images for a one off fee. Höppner will be launching in late 2018, but you're welcome to sign up now!

Pricing

At Höppner, we have multiple pricing options to suit your needs. Pricing starts from $5 per photo, but gets cheaper per photo the more you buy in the same transaction. We also offer a highly affordable subscription package for developers that will be using our service frequently. The subsciption option offer features unlimited photos for $20 per month.
